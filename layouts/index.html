About Us

We're two moms with young kids who want structure this summer!
Bring your kids and enjoy playmates and adult conversation at our park meetups.
- Sara and Marie

 

Tuesdays: parks local to Fort Collins, sometimes paired with other events nearby like storytimes. 9:30 am-11:30 am

Fridays: bigger parks in Fort Collins and surrounding areas. Most will have a water option: splash pad, pond, creek, etc. We recommend you give your kids the gift of time and plan to stay the whole morning! 9:30 am - 1:30pm

Helpful things to pack, depending on the park

lunch sunscreen
water bottles shade umbrella
swim suits sand / water toys
critter-catching nets snacks
change of clothes wipes (helpful at any age amiright)
